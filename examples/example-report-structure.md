## SECTION 1: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

> In April 2026, CPI Bangladesh's Health Outreach Programme (HOP) conducted 4,250 household visits across Camp 1W (Blocks A & B) and Camp 4 (Blocks D & F), reaching 12,750 individuals with health education, screening, and referral services. Key achievements include: (1) 95% of monthly household visit target achieved, (2) 312 referrals made to the CPI Health Post, and (3) 8 community health education sessions conducted. A challenge with CHW availability during Ramadan was mitigated through adjusted shift scheduling.

---

## SECTION 2: PROGRAM CONTEXT

- **Target Population:** Rohingya refugees in Camp 1W (Blocks A, B) and Camp 4 (Blocks D, F)
- **Population Served:** ~24,000 individuals (estimated catchment)
- **CHW Team:** 12 Community Health Workers + 2 Senior CHWs + 1 Field Coordinator
- **Operational Period:** 1–30 April 2026

---

## SECTION 3: KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

### 3.1 Household Visits
CHWs completed 4,250 household visits against a monthly target of 4,500 (94.4%). Coverage was slightly reduced during the first week of Ramadan.

### 3.2 Referrals
312 referrals made to CPI Health Post, with 89% follow-up completion. Top referral reasons: fever (28%), cough/respiratory (22%), diarrhea (15%).

### 3.3 Health Education Sessions
8 group sessions conducted on: hygiene promotion (3), nutrition (2), vaccination awareness (2), family planning (1). Total attendees: 186 (124 women, 62 men).

## SECTION 6: FIELD STORY

> **Rahima's Story: A Mother's Journey to Health**
>
> Rahima, a mother of three in Camp 1W, had been experiencing persistent fever and fatigue. During a routine household visit, CPI CHW Fatema recognized the symptoms and referred Rahima to the Health Post, where she was diagnosed with malaria and treated. "I didn't realize how sick I was," Rahima said. "The CHW comes to my door. She knows my children. She cares."
>
> Rahima has since recovered fully and now attends health education sessions regularly. She has become a hygiene promoter in her block, sharing handwashing practices with neighbors.
