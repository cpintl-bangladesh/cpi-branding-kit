## POST DESIGN TEMPLATES

### 1. Stat / Key Achievement Post (Square 1080×1080)
```
BACKGROUND: CPI Red (#D91E4D) OR CPI Purple (#41273B)
TOP-LEFT: CPI Logo white version, height ~60px
MAIN CONTENT (centered):
  Number/stat: Arial Bold or Raleway Bold, 80–100pt, White
  Context label: Arial Regular, 22–28pt, White
BOTTOM BAR: Dark overlay strip
  "Empower communities. Transform lives." — 16pt, White
  www.cpintl.org — 12pt, White at 70% opacity
```

### 2. Impact Story Photo Post (Square 1080×1080)
```
BACKGROUND: Full-bleed CPI-approved community photo
OVERLAY: CPI Red or Purple gradient from bottom, 50% opacity
TOP: CPI Logo white, top-left, 60px height
BOTTOM TEXT AREA:
  Headline: Arial Bold, 24–28pt, White
  One-liner: Arial Regular, 18pt, White
  "Read more at cpintl.org" — Arial Regular, 14pt, CPI Light Grey
```

### 3. Quote / Testimonial Post (Square 1080×1080)
```
BACKGROUND: White OR CPI Light Grey (#D0C4C5)
TOP ACCENT: CPI Red bar, full width, 80px height
  "Quote or Story" — Arial Bold, 16pt, White
LARGE QUOTATION MARK: CPI Red, very large, 180pt, top-left of quote
QUOTE TEXT: Arial Semi-Bold or Raleway Semi-Bold, 22–26pt, CPI Purple
ATTRIBUTION: Arial Regular, 16pt, CPI Mid Grey
  "— [Name], [Role], [Location]"
BOTTOM: CPI Logo + website
```

### 4. Event Announcement Post (Landscape 1200×630)
```
LEFT PANEL (40%): CPI Red background
  CPI Logo white, top-center of panel
  "EVENT" or "TRAINING" — Arial Bold, 18pt, White, uppercase
  Event Title: Arial Bold, 28pt, White
  Date: Arial Regular, 20pt, White
  Location: Arial Regular, 16pt, CPI Light Grey
RIGHT PANEL (60%): Photo or White background
  Supporting image or content details
```

### 5. Program Update / Newsletter Header (Email, 600px wide)
```
TOP: CPI Red bar (#D91E4D), full width, 80px
  CPI Logo white centered OR left-aligned
SUBHEADER: CPI Purple bar, full width, 40px
  "BANGLADESH MISSION UPDATE | [Month Year]" — Arial Bold, 12pt, White
BODY: White background, Arial Regular 14pt, CPI Black
  Standard newsletter content
FOOTER: CPI Purple bar + contacts + unsubscribe
```

### Impact Achievement:
```

[2–3 sentences: what happened, who it impacted, why it matters]

### Field Story:
```

[2–3 sentences telling the story]

[Impact statement: what changed because of this work]

📸 Photo: [Photographer name] / CPI
🌐 Full story: cpintl.org

#CPI #Bangladesh #HealthOutreach #[RelevantHashtag]

```

🌐 www.cpintl.org
#CPIBangladesh #[Program] #[Thematic]
```

## SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS

### DO:
- ✅ Always include CPI logo on designed graphics
- ✅ Use approved CPI colors only
- ✅ Credit photographers on photos: *Photo: [Name] / CPI*
- ✅ Show communities in dignity and empowerment
- ✅ Use real names and stories (with proper consent)
- ✅ Tag partners when appropriate (YPSA, Health Sector, etc.)
- ✅ Include call to action (link, donate, learn more)

### DON'T:
- ❌ Post photos of individuals without consent
- ❌ Use unapproved fonts or colors
- ❌ Post content that depicts suffering or victimhood
- ❌ Use the logo on busy photographic backgrounds
- ❌ Make claims without evidence
- ❌ Use the same graphic twice without updating date/context
- ❌ Post without reviewing for spelling and fact accuracy

## EMAIL NEWSLETTER TEMPLATE STRUCTURE

```
[HEADER — 600px wide]
  CPI Red bar (80px) + White CPI Logo centered
  CPI Purple bar (40px) + "NEWSLETTER TITLE | Month Year" white Arial Bold

[LEAD STORY — 600px]
  Full-width photo (600×300px)
  Headline: Arial Bold, 22pt, CPI Purple
  Body: Arial Regular, 14pt, CPI Black, 1.6 line spacing
  "Read more →" link: CPI Red

[STAT ROW — 600px, 3 columns]
  Each column (180px): CPI Red background
  Stat: Arial Bold, 36pt, White
  Label: Arial Regular, 12pt, White

[PROGRAM UPDATE BLOCK]
  Left icon (60px) + Right text
  Icon: CPI Red circle with white icon
  Heading: Arial Bold, 16pt, CPI Purple
  Body: Arial Regular, 13pt, CPI Black

[FIELD STORY / QUOTE]
  Light grey background
  Large CPI Red quotation mark
  Quote: Arial Semi-Bold, 16pt, CPI Purple
  Attribution: Arial Regular, 12pt, CPI Mid Grey

[FOOTER — 600px]
  CPI Purple background
  CPI Logo white (100px wide)
  Contact info: Arial Regular, 11pt, White
  Social icons (CPI Red outlined circles)
  Unsubscribe | Privacy Policy | View online
```
