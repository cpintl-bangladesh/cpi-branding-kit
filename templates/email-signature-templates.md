# CPI Email Signature Templates
## Community Partners International — Compliant Email Signatures

---

## HOW TO USE

1. Copy the HTML signature into your email client's signature settings
2. Replace `[Full Name]`, `[Job Title]`, `[Program]`, `[Mobile]`, `[Email]`
3. Upload the CPI logo PNG (white background — max 120px wide)
4. Test by sending yourself a test email

## EMAIL SIGNATURE RULES

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Font | Arial (system font for email compatibility) |
| Name | Arial Bold, CPI Purple `#41273B`, 11pt |
| Title | Arial Regular, CPI Black `#2D2926`, 10pt |
| Contact details | Arial Regular, CPI Mid Grey `#948794`, 9pt |
| Divider line | 1px solid CPI Red `#D91E4D` |
| Logo | PNG or SVG, max 120px wide, linked to cpintl.org |
| Tagline | Arial Italic, CPI Red `#D91E4D`, 9pt |
| Max width | 500–600px |
| Background | Transparent (no colored backgrounds) |
