## OPERATIONAL CONTEXT

### Population Served
- **Primary:** Rohingya refugee population — approximately 970,000+ refugees in 33 camps in Cox's Bazar District (UN estimate)
- **Secondary:** Bangladeshi host communities in Cox's Bazar District
- **Tertiary:** Patuakhali host communities (WASH & Clean Energy)

### Camp Focus
- Camp 1W (Blocks A & B) — Health Post UID 418, Health Outreach
- Camp 4 (Blocks D & F) — Health Outreach, Scabies MDA
- Camp 5 — NCD Corner operations (RTMI partnership)
- Camp 17 — NCD Corner operations (RTMI partnership)

### Rohingya Crisis Key Facts
| Fact | Figure |
|------|--------|
| Total Rohingya in Bangladesh | ~970,000+ (UN estimate) |
| Arrived in 2017 mass displacement | 740,000+ |
| Camps in Cox's Bazar | 33 |
| Camp area | ~24 km² |
| Population density | 47,000/km² |
| Children among refugees | >50% |
| Monthly WFP ration (2025) | $6/person (halved) |
| New arrivals Jan 2024–Jul 2025 | 150,000+ |

## ALL PROGRAMS

### 1. Health Outreach Programme (HOP)
- CHW-based household outreach across Camps 1W and 4
- Doorstep screening, EPI referrals, ANC/FBD referrals
- Hygiene distribution, health education
- Facility UID: HF-418 | Partner: YPSA

### 2. Health Post Program (HP)
- Static facility (UID 418) in Camp 1W, B15 Block
- Serves 34,000+ refugees and host community members
- OPD, emergency stabilization, reproductive care, MHPSS
- NCD Corner, AMR diagnostics
- Partner: YPSA

### 3. Hepatitis C Virus (HCV) Program
- WHO 90-90-90 cascade: test, treat, cure
- DAA treatment, SVR monitoring
- CHW-led community mobilization
- CVD-HCV cRCT with JHU, icddr,b, RTMI

### 4. CVD/NCD Program
- Hypertension, diabetes screening and management
- NCD Corners in Camps 5 and 17 (RTMI)
- Cluster RCT evaluating CHW-led CVD model
- WHO PEN guideline compliance

### 5. Health System Strengthening (HSS)
- Sadar Hospital ED support — Phase 10 MoU (2026)
- 5 MBBS Medical Doctors, IPC staff, lab reagents
- Partner capacity building
- Focal points: Dr. Md. Minhajur Rahman, Dr. Sabuktagin Mahmud Shohel

### 6. WASH & Clean Energy
- Twin-pit latrines, bathing cubicles, solar lighting (Camps 1W, 4, 17)
- Patuakhali: WCEP Phase 3 with SDA
- Mini-piped water networks, biogas plants
- CPIL social enterprise O&M model

### 7. Education Program
- Learning for Future (L4F) with Educo
- Camps 1W, 4, 17: 8 ECE centers, 4 LCs, 4 CBLFs
- 440 learners
- Life-skills, child safeguarding, PSEA training

### 8. Livelihood & Alternative Energy
- Biogas plants in Patuakhali host communities
- Organic waste to cooking fuel
- Water management committees, O&M jobs
- Partner: SDA

### 9. Emergency Preparedness
- Community-based disaster response teams
- Fire, flood, and cyclone response in Cox's Bazar camps

## ZarishSphere HIS Reference
CPI Bangladesh is developing **ZarishSphere** — a national-grade Health Information System for the Rohingya refugee response:
- **Architecture:** Connect+Extend federated model
- **FCN:** Universal patient identifier across all facilities
- **Facility UID:** HF-418 (anchor)
- **DHIS2 Integration:** Under YPSA credentials
- **390-item requirements checklist** across 25 system domains
- All ZarishSphere branded materials should follow CPI brand standards with the ZarishSphere name as a program identifier beneath the CPI brand mark
