## PRIMARY DESIGN RULES (NEVER VIOLATE)

1. **Never place the logo on a colored background** (exception: CPI brand background colors only)
2. **Never rotate, distort, stretch, or change logo proportions**
3. **Never change logo colors** — use only approved versions
4. **Never use logo below minimum size** (0.5cm primary, 0.9cm secondary, 0.8cm with tagline)
5. **Never place logo on busy/photographic backgrounds**
6. **Never change the font of the logotype**
7. **Always maintain clear space** equal to the x-width of the "P" in the logo
8. **CPI Red (#D91E4D) is the primary accent** — use it for headlines, tagline, icons
9. **CPI Purple (#41273B) is the primary dark** — use it for body text in branded materials
10. **Raleway is the primary typeface** for all external communications
