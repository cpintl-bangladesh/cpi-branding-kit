## NAME CARD — BANGLADESH

**Actual Size:** W 90 × H 55 mm (standard business card)  
**Bleed:** 3mm on all sides  
**Resolution:** 300 DPI for print

### Layout Structure:

**FRONT:**
- Top-left: CPI Primary Logo (full color, min 0.8cm height)
- Watermark: CPI butterfly mark, bottom-right, grey at 46% opacity
- Tagline: *Empower communities. Transform lives.* — bottom-left, Raleway Medium, CPI Purple or Red, 6pt

**BACK:**
- Name: Raleway Bold, 8pt, CPI Purple
- Position Title: Raleway Regular, 6pt, CPI Purple
- Divider line: 0.5pt, CPI Red
- Details block (Raleway Regular + Semi-Bold, 6pt):
  - 📞 Mobile number
  - 📞 Office phone
  - ✉ email@cpintl.org
  - 🌐 www.cpintl.org
  - 📍 Address
- CPI Bangladesh Address block:
  ```
  Community Partners International — Bangladesh Mission
  Country Office: Navana Bilkis Laurent, Gulshan-2, Dhaka-1212
  Project Office: White House (3rd Floor), Kolatoli Bypass Road, Cox's Bazar 4700
  Email: cpi.bangladesh@cpintl.org
  ```
- Bottom strip: CPI USA address in very small text (6pt) + CPI logo (right-aligned)

## PRESENTATION / SLIDE DECK TEMPLATE

### Cover Slide:
- Full-bleed CPI program photo OR solid CPI Purple background
- CPI Logo: top-left, white reversed version
- Title: Raleway Bold, 36–44pt, White
- Subtitle/date: Raleway Light, 18–22pt, White or CPI Light Grey
- CPI Red horizontal band at bottom with tagline in white

### Section Divider Slide:
- Background: CPI Red OR CPI Purple solid fill
- Section number: Raleway Light, 80pt, White at 20% opacity
- Section title: Raleway Bold, 36pt, White

### Content Slide:
- Background: White
- Header bar: CPI Red `#D91E4D`, height 8mm, full width
- Slide title: Raleway Bold, 24pt, White (inside red bar)
- Body: Arial Regular, 16–18pt, CPI Black
- Footer: CPI logo bottom-right + page number, small

### Data/Chart Slide:
- Use CPI color palette for charts (Red, Purple, Blue, Mid Grey, Light Grey)
- Chart title: Raleway Semi-Bold, 16pt, CPI Purple
- Data labels: Arial Bold, 12pt, matching bar/segment color or White
- Source note: Arial Regular, 9pt, CPI Mid Grey

## REPORT TEMPLATE STRUCTURE

### Cover:
- Full-bleed photo with CPI Red or Purple overlay
- CPI logo (white) top-left
- Report title: Raleway Bold, 36–48pt, White
- Subtitle/period: Raleway Light, 18pt, White
- CPI Bangladesh or Program name: Raleway Semi-Bold, 14pt, White
- Month/Year: Raleway Regular, 12pt, White

### Inside Pages:
- **Margin:** 20mm all sides
- **Running header:** CPI Red thin bar (6mm) with report title in White Raleway Semi-Bold, 10pt + CPI logo bottom-right corner
- **Section headings:** Raleway Bold, 16pt, CPI Red
- **Sub-headings:** Raleway Semi-Bold, 13pt, CPI Purple
- **Body:** Arial Regular, 11pt, CPI Black, 1.5 line spacing
- **Tables:** Header row in CPI Purple with white Arial Bold 10pt; Alternating rows White and CPI Light Grey `#D0C4C5`
- **Charts:** CPI color palette (see brand-colors.json)
- **Callout boxes:** Use styles from design-elements.md

### Back Cover:
- CPI Purple or Red full-bleed
- White CPI Logo centered or bottom-right
- Contact information white text
- Website + social handles
